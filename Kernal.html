What is a Kernel?

The kernel is the central component of a computer operating systems. The only job performed by the kernel is to the manage the communication between
the software and the hardware. A Kernel is at the nucleus of a computer. It makes the communication between the hardware and software possible. While
the Kernel is the innermost part of an operating system, a shell is the outermost one.

Features of Kennel
